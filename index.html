WELCOME TO THE ALPHABET GAME

This game is a mix between the famous spanish game "Pasapalabra", broadcast on television for years, and the original british comedy panel game show The Alphabet Game.

The rules are easy: in the letters-wheel, gameplay proceeds through letters of the alphabet. For each letter, will appear a word definition starting with or containing that letter. The aim is to respond with the correct word the current definition. In case of doubt, it's possible to pass to the next definition by typing "next" and come back when the wheel is completed to have another chance to answer.

The game happens against the clock, and to win, it's necessary to succeed with all answers and make no failures before the time is up.
